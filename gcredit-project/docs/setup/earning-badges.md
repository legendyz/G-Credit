# How to Earn Badges

Welcome to the G-Credit Badge System! This guide explains how you can earn digital credentials that recognize your skills and achievements.

## What are Badges?

Badges are digital credentials that certify your completion of training programs, demonstrate specific skills, or recognize significant achievements. Each badge includes:

- **Verified Information**: Badge issuer, issue date, and criteria met
- **Public Verification**: Shareable verification URL for third parties
- **Skills Recognition**: Associated skills and competencies
- **Permanent Record**: Stored securely in your badge wallet

## How to Earn Badges

### 1. Complete Training Programs

Most badges are awarded upon successful completion of internal or external training programs:

- **Online Courses**: Complete courses on platforms like Microsoft Learn, Coursera, or internal LMS
- **Workshops**: Attend and participate in skill-building workshops
- **Certifications**: Obtain industry-recognized certifications

### 2. Achieve Milestones

Automatic badges may be awarded when you reach certain milestones:

- **First Badge Earned**: Welcome to the badge community!
- **5 Badges Earned**: Badge Collector milestone
- **10 Badges Earned**: Badge Expert milestone

### 3. Demonstrate Skills

Show your skills in action through:

- **Project Completion**: Successfully deliver complex projects
- **Peer Recognition**: Receive nominations from colleagues
- **Manager Endorsement**: Get endorsed by your manager for specific competencies

## Claiming Your Badges

When you earn a badge, you'll receive:

1. **Email Notification**: Check your inbox for the badge claim notification
2. **Claim Link**: Click the secure link to claim your badge (valid for 7 days)
3. **Badge Wallet**: Your claimed badge appears in your badge wallet

## Sharing Your Badges

Once claimed, you can:

- **Download**: Get PNG or JSON-LD files for your badge
- **Share on LinkedIn**: Add to your profile to showcase achievements
- **Verify**: Share the public verification URL with recruiters or colleagues
- **Display**: Use badge images in email signatures or resumes

## Badge Categories

Explore available badges in these categories:

- **Technical Skills**: Programming, data analysis, cloud computing
- **Leadership**: Management, project management, team building
- **Professional Development**: Communication, time management, creativity
- **Compliance**: Security training, ethics, regulatory requirements

## Getting Started

Ready to start earning badges?

1. Visit the [Badge Catalog](/badges/templates) to see available badges
2. Review the earning criteria for badges that interest you
3. Enroll in relevant training programs or projects
4. Complete the requirements and wait for your badge notification!
