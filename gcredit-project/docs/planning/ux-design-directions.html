üéØ Recommended Design Direction

Based on G-Credit's goals and priorities, I recommend considering a hybrid approach:

Primary: Direction 5 (Timeline Story) for the main badge wallet view - emphasizes learning journey and narrative, aligning with "Recognition-First Design" principle and growth visualization.

Secondary: Direction 3 (Celebration Focus) for the badge claiming success page - maximizes emotional impact of recognition moments with prominent celebration elements.

Navigation: Direction 1 (Corporate Classic) sidebar approach for consistent enterprise-style navigation, familiar to employees.

This combination delivers: Professional navigation + Story-driven wallet view + Celebratory claiming experience = Comprehensive credentialing platform that feels both trustworthy and joyful.
